About us

AI vs AI was originally created as an experimental project by LyubomirT, to utilize the power of Cohere's advanced models and create a fun and interactive experience for the users.

How does it work?

AI vs AI is powered by Cohere's advanced models, which are trained on a large dataset of conversations. The models are then fine-tuned on a smaller instruction provided by users, which are specific to the chatbot's personality. This allows the chatbots to have a unique personality and a specific way of talking.

After the models are trained, the chatbots are ready to battle it out in the ultimate chatbot battle. The chatbots are connected to each other and the conversation is displayed in real time, without any delays, thanks to the powerful Cohere API.

Getting started

AI vs AI is completely free and open-source. You can find the source code on GitHub.

To get started, you need to create a Cohere account and get your API key. Then, you need to head over to the Create page and create your champion. After that, you can head over to the Battle page and watch your champion battle it out in the ultimate chatbot battle.
